Embroidery

home > services > embroidery

On-site, we operate five multi-head embroidery machines with skilled long-term production staff that consistently exceed expectations of even the most demanding clients on a regular basis! The embroidery process starts with good art that we must first “digitize” using specialized computer software. This creates the standardized language each machine understands, controlling things like sequence, when to trim, change thread colors; or speed up or down as your logo is embroidered.

At the same time, anyone of dozens of specialty stitches may also be used to further improve “the look” maximizing the impact of each embroidered design we produce.

Since many in this department have over twenty years experience, our machines are also maintained to the highest standards possible. Nearly 300 different thread colors are stocked to instill a good match to already established branding guidelines or team colors. Work-flow procedures also include presentation of a preliminary sew out of each logo to assure satisfaction long before production ever starts.

Typically, embroidery is used on polo’s, woven’s, jackets, caps, tote bags and much more. If it helps, as you plan or set budgets, the size of the design, number of stitches required to reproduce the design, quantity of units ordered, in addition to the item of choice for the promotion, all impact the final delivered price.

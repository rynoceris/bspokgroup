Laser Engraving

home > services > laser engraving

Laser Engraving is the newest form of engraving which offers exceptional detail, and the type of finish in this category done most often by modern engravers. The laser is more versatile, more accurate, faster and more economical than any other type of engraving; personalization’s are far easier to accomplish on the laser than nearly any other form of embellishment besides direct to garment [DTG]. We can even engrave photographs on many items using our lasers! Laser engraving offers lots of flexibility in reproducing artwork, small fonts and tight copy on most products. One of the few drawbacks to lasers comes when engraving glass. With glass though, the high heat involved can crack or shatter the glass if too much is applied, so lasers are only able to etch the surface of the glass rather than deep engrave it.

Laser Engraving is one of the best solutions for creating a permanent marking which does not detract from the appearance of the item. By varying both the speed and power of engraving, we are able to make engraving barely visible, but can also make it large and noticeable, if desired. Many different materials can be engraved or marked with a laser, including anodized aluminum. It is also possible to engrave other materials via the use of a laser; materials such as plastic, wood, leather and glass. Engraving is useful for many purposes, from creating aesthetic designs on promotional products to very unique business cards and even custom Christmas ornaments! The possibilities are nearly endless!

Privacy Policy

home > privacy policy

Modern information gathering is crucial to the operation of our Internet order fulfillment activities, and your privacy is honored and valued. This notice describes the privacy policy for bspok group, a USA based company.

You can access and browse our website without disclosing any personal information. Our web site does not enable any communication with other visitors or to post information that can be accessed by others.

All personal information collected is volunteered by our visitors. We do not research data about our visitors from other sources, such as public records or private organizations. The information is used in the fulfillment of order confirmation, correspondence in reference to these orders if needed, and order fulfillment. This information is not shared, rented, or sold to any other party or group. Any promotional material you would receive by mail or email would be from us and only for items specifically similar to those you've already expressed an interest in. To have yourself removed from our mailing lists you can call 800-433-7289, or to have yourself removed from our emailing list automatically Click Here.

Some information on our visitors is gathered automatically such as their web browser type, the point where they entered our site, and number of page views. This information is used for analysis of our web site traffic and is in no way linked with personal information that is manually entered. Collection of information in this way is done with "cookies". To disable the use of cookies or to have your computer notify you when a new cookie is being received consult the Help menu on your web browser's toolbar.

We do not knowingly collect information from children, and our site is not designed for interaction with children.

Our internal security policies and technical measures protect the personal data we have from unauthorized access, improper use or disclosure, unauthorized modification, and unlawful destruction or accidental loss. All employees and data processors that have access to the use of personal data are obligated to respect the confidentiality of our visitors' personal data. We ensure that your personal data will not be disclosed to State institutions or authorities except, and only, if required by law or other regulation.

To access, update, delete, or obtain a copy of the information we have for you email customerservice@bspokgroup.com, call 800-433-7289, or write to us at 2240 Distributors Drive, Indianapolis, IN 46241. We reserve the right to refuse to divulge information in the interest of security or policy, but we will demonstrate clear reason for such a decision.

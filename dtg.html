Direct to Garment Printing ["DTG"]

home > services > direct to garment

Direct to Garment [DTG] printing is a new state of the art technology to the screen-printing industry that has grown in popularity and preference within the past ten years. As of 2007, [DTG] printing was so new that 99% of printing was traditional and 1% [DTG] on textiles! Direct to garment [DTG] is the process of printing directly onto apparel using specialized inkjet printers engineered specifically for applications on textiles. The better [DTG] printers provide a clean, easy and convenient way to decorate garments replacing conventional screen-printing and dye sublimation methods.

In the last five years, as consumer choices have changed, the emphasis and focus on personalization has continued to increase annually, too. recognizes customer's are also expecting greater control in design process that we want to support. [DTG] printers have undergone a revolution as they have completely changed. [DTG] manufacturers recognized the steady shift by consumers and listened to the printing professionals. Over the past few years, companies like bspōk group made it clear to them the personalization, increased individuality, and expression trend showed no sign any time soon of changing or going away.

You’re probably thinking, “What’s the big deal and why does this benefit me?

With traditional screen-printing, budgets are often limited on the amount of colors you can have per design, even though some of the largest industrial shops may have the newest fourteen color automatic presses. This is due to costly set-up and screen charges when factored into small print runs to determine a final piece price. If you have a very detailed logo with lots of colors [like a picture image] that you need printed, or low quantities, direct to garment [DTG] printing over traditional screen-printing may be the best way to go!

Here are some helpful tips to get you started:

To get the very best digital print, quality artwork is one of the most important factors when considering direct to garment [DTG] printing. welcome’s complex CMYK artwork for even those smaller six piece runs. Afterall, that’s what the [DTG] printer is for! In the long run, if you really do only need a small run of shirts printed, then it will likely be far less expensive to have them printed digitally.

For [DTG] printing the best possible prints come from artwork prepared in the following manner:

  • CMYK PSD File 300 dpi.
  • Along with a transparent background, all layers should be merged together as one.
  • Artwork MUST also be sized to print before it is submitted, with all colors adjusted as you see fit.

To avoid additional charges all these requirements must be fulfilled in order to proceed quickly and decisively to process your artwork.

Remember: Artwork preparation is the most vital step in ensuring that your final product is as vibrant and stunning as it can possibly be. If artwork is not to size, there is no transparent background, or any other requirements are not met, we will return your file to you for changes. Or, if at that point you prefer: Our graphic design department can process your artwork, which does add additional art charges.

If you are familiar with Adobe ® Photoshop, when considering digital photography also suggests use of the BRIGHTNESS / CONTRAST sliders to add greater contrast to your images. This will help to bring out the brightest possible colors in your prints.

Other good tools are the FOREGROUND FILL (to replace duller areas of color with brighter shades of the same color), CURVES, COLOR BALANCE, and HUE / SATURATION. The goal is to make the image a little brighter than you actually want it to print (without washing out the color and detail, and ensuring you are in CMYK mode when you do it). This will ensure that the shirt comes out to your satisfaction the first time we print it.
